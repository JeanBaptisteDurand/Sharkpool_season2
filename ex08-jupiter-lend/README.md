# Exercise 08 - Jupiter Lend Deposit

## Task

Deposit any amount of stablecoins into Jupiter Lend.

## Instructions

This must be done through the Jupiter web app on **mainnet**.

### Steps

1. **Go to** [jup.ag/lend](https://jup.ag/lend)
2. **Connect your wallet** (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
3. **Choose a stablecoin vault** — e.g. USDC (3.68% APY), USDT (2.6%), or USDG (5.37%)
4. **Click on the vault** to open the deposit panel
5. **Enter the amount** you want to deposit (any amount works)
6. **Click "Deposit"** and approve the transaction in your wallet
7. **Copy the TX hash** from the confirmation or wallet history

### Tips

- You need stablecoins (USDC, USDT, etc.) in your wallet
- Any amount works, even $1
- You can withdraw freely at any time
- Deposits earn passive yield automatically

## Answer

Paste the TX hash / Solscan link on the site.
